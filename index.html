The Restaurant

At Sushi Katsu, you can enjoy a variety of fresh, quality sushi and Japanese dishes. Our customers say that we offer a sushi experience like no other.

Our beautiful atomsphere and ambiance is superb for friends, family, couples and big groups. We offer authentic Japanese dishes with a mouth-watering menu that's sure to please the most discriminating taste buds.

There's something for everyone, so if sushi isn't your thing you can pick from other items. If you are undecided upon looking at our vast menu, Katsu friendly, professional hosts can recommend you a tasty pick to make this a pleasurable experience!

Come visit us soon and experience Japanese cuisine and fusion at its best.
